The Nigerian off-grid opportunity

Over 180 million Nigerians are either off-grid or experience unreliable grid service. The economy is powered largely by small-scale generators (10â€“15 GW).
As a result, Nigerian households and businesses spend almost $14 billion annually on expensive and inefficient generation at $0.40/kWh or more. These generators emit noise and air pollution which kills thousands of Nigerian families every year.
Providing access to off-grid solar home systems (SHS) alternatives to complement the unreliable grid creates a $2B/YR market opportunity that will help Nigerian homes and small businesses save over 20% of their monthly income and improve the quality of lives.
