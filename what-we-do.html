WHAT WE DO

The scale of problem

The Nigerian off-grid solar opportunity

Over 180 million Nigerians are either off-grid or experience unreliable grid service. The economy is powered largely by small-scale generators (10–15 GW). As a result, Nigerian households and businesses spend almost $14 billion annually on expensive and inefficient generation at $0.40/kWh or more. These generators emit noise and air pollution which kills thousands of Nigerian families every year.

Providing access to off-grid solar home systems (SHS) alternatives to complement the unreliable grid creates a $2B/YR market opportunity that will help Nigerian homes and small businesses save over 20% of their monthly income. Therefore, we are kickstarting our platform by providing access and affordability to clean, reliable and affordable solar electricity for poor families because we know that access to stable electricity doesn’t just improve education, health, employment and environmental outcomes, it also helps to break the poor out of the poverty cycle.

The ecosystem opportunity

The Nigerian off-grid solar market is still at a nascent stage of only 5% market penetration. It faces diverse problems like major competition from mini-generators, low consumer awareness and purchasing power, shifting patterns of demand, fluctuations in exchange rates, high upfront cost of product financing, fragmented data, low profit margins and unsustainable operation costs, regulatory lags, etc. – it is a complex problem.

However, the market has huge growth potential for any company that understands the intricacies of the market, ecosystem and country – which we do.

Our solution

We strongly believe the low market penetration of off-grid solar is largely due to the silo approach by most industry stakeholders. To escalate market penetration, there has to be a collaborative effort by a diverse group of key stakeholders to create a family of solutions to raise awareness, stimulate demand, and build the last-mile distribution and economic infrastructure to train and support customers, sales agents, regulators, and other contributors.

Veritas Vision seeks to be the catalyst in that ecosystem. With our understanding of the market and collaborative culture, we are building a suite of solutions to provide the ecosystem with:

Information

Our last-mile distributed network of micro-social-entrepreneurs trained to be relationship agents who educate poor families on the benefits of renewable energy.

Products

Curated and bespoke solar solutions designed to solve the unique energy needs of our beneficiaries.

Finance

Affordable asset financing options at bespoke interest rates and comfortable payment plans.

Support

A community designed to engage, educate and support our customers throughout and beyond the lifecycle of the products they buy through us.

Education

An inclusive educational platform to deliver bespoke training for last-mile sales agents for the off-grid solar industry and other stakeholders.
